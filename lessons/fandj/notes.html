Master The F & J Home Row Lesson

This is the first and most important typing lesson. The F and J keys are called home row anchor keys. Your fingers always return here. Without mastering these keys, typing speed and accuracy will always remain unstable.

Why F & J Keys Matter

The F and J keys contain small bumps on a real keyboard. These bumps help your fingers identify the correct hand position without looking at the keyboard. Your left index finger stays on F. Your right index finger stays on J. This creates muscle memory.

Left Hand

Keep your left index finger relaxed on the F key. Do not lift the whole hand.

Right Hand

Your right index finger should rest naturally on J. Avoid pressing too hard.

Space Bar

Use your thumb gently for space. Typing should feel smooth, not forced.
